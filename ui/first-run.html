Download the Native Client

How to Pin YT Downloader to your toolbar

To make YT Downloader easily accessible, pin it to your Firefox toolbar:

  1. Click the Extensions button at the top right of your browser.
    This might be hidden in the Hamburger menu ≡
  2. Find YT Downloader in the list.
  3. Right click, and click the Pin to Toolbar button.

(Optional) You can Right Click the addon, click 'Manage Extension', then Allow to Run in Private Windows.

What's next? Finish the setup in the Settings page!

  • You now must check that all Dependencies are installed.
  • You can also change your download location, and enable desktop notifications for when downloads complete!
