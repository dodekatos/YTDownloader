Video / Audio Re-encoding Tool

You can use this to re-encode videos or audio into different codecs / formats, making use of FFmpeg entirely locally on your PC.
I've tried to foolproof it as much as I could, but it does require an ounce of thinking.

Note: The output file size for some high quality videos might be higher than the source video, I'll fix this in a future update. You should check this after encoding.
